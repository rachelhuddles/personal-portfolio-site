Sweet by Nature, Inc.

Employee Data Center

An online portal built for the employee's of Sweet by Nature, Inc. This site was built as part of APSU's senior Systems Analysis & Design course, in which students partner with companies to build automated systems that replace the company's existing manual systems. The Employee Data Center allows Sweet by Nature, Inc. employees to store all critical inventory, customer, and sales information in a database, rather than tracking the information manually. The Data Center also provides the employees with several new, useful data-based reports that provide valuable insight to the business.

View a video demo of the data center below! To protect proprietary information, all data shown in the video demo and on the sample website has been changed.
