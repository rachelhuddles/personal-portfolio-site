About Me

Hi! I'm Rachel. With a strong foundation in computer science, I'm passionate about designing and building high-quality, intuitive, exceptional applications. My work focuses on how we store and interact with data - including database development, data analysis, and building polished, excellent applications used to interact with databases.

I am an ICCP Certified Data Professional and graduated with a degree in Computer Information Technology with a study concentration in Database Administration. My collegiate coursework focused on the design, implementation, and administration of databases, including those accessed by Web applications.

I am naturally curious and love learning new things. In my spare time, I enjoy taking extra professional development courses to help cultivate my interests and have earned several certificates in Data Analysis, UX, and Web Design.

Thank you for taking the time to check out my site! Have a look around and enjoy seeing what I have created!
