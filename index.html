ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Rachel and I'm a Computer Science and Information Technology student. Here are a few things you should know about me:

I am currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Information Technology at Austin Peay State University. My study concentration is Database Administration, which trains students in the design, implementation, and administration of databases, including those accessed by Web applications.

I am naturally curious and love learning new things. In my spare time, I enjoy taking extra professional development courses to help cultivate my interests. Currently, I am most interested in algorithms and machine learning.

I have a second Bachelor's degree in Child Development and Learning. I spent a year as an elementary school teacher prior to pursuing this Computer Science and Information Technology degree. It was a desire to create apps, websites, and educational software for my own classroom that sparked my interest in coding and technology. I still work with elementary-aged kids, including directing and leading a STEM camp. I remain passionate about spreading the joy of learning and creating an interest in the limitless possibilities of technology!

Thank you for taking the time to check out my site! Have a look around and enjoy seeing what I have created!
